# Cold War (c. 1947–1991)

## Quick Summary
A prolonged geopolitical, ideological, technological, and proxy conflict between the US-led and USSR-led blocs.

## Core Drivers
- Ideological rivalry (capitalism/liberal democracy vs state socialism)
- Security dilemma after WWII
- Nuclear deterrence and arms race logic

## Key Patterns
- Proxy wars (Korea, Vietnam, Afghanistan, etc.)
- Intelligence/espionage competition
- Space race and missile crises
- Alliance systems: NATO vs Warsaw Pact

## Outcomes
- Soviet collapse (1991) and end of bipolar structure
- Expansion of globalized market systems
- Persistent nuclear deterrence architecture

## Mini Timeline
- 1947: Truman Doctrine / Marshall Plan era
- 1948–49: Berlin Blockade/Airlift
- 1962: Cuban Missile Crisis
- 1989: Fall of Berlin Wall
- 1991: USSR dissolves
